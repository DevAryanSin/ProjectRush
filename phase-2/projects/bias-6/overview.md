This project delivers a Next.js 16 web application, RedTeamAI, designed to combat algorithmic bias. Leveraging a Claymorphism UI and Gemini's AI capabilities, it allows users to input details about their AI systems. The application then generates adversarial bias attack scenarios to identify and flag potential discrimination, promoting fairness and accountability in AI. It's a production-ready MVP focused on accessibility and clear visualization of potential AI flaws.
