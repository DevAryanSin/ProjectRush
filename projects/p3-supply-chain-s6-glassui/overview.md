CargoDebrief is a Next.js 16 application designed to conduct forensic post-mortem analysis of supply chain disruptions. Leveraging Google Gemini AI, it generates comprehensive reports including root cause analysis, systemic weaknesses, lessons learned, and a prioritized prevention checklist, helping organizations swiftly mitigate future delays and enhance logistics resilience. The system provides a premium, "Glass UI" experience, making complex data actionable and visually accessible.
