# Brief
The proliferation of digital sports media across global platforms creates a significant visibility gap for rights holders, leaving proprietary content vulnerable to unauthorized use and intellectual property violations. DMCA Forge provides a scalable, innovative solution to combat this by streamlining the process of generating formal DMCA takedown notices, enabling rapid response to infringements.

# Opportunities
## Differentiation
DMCA Forge stands out with its AI-powered, instant legal drafting capabilities, specifically tailored for the digital sports media domain. Its unique Flat Design UI prioritizes clarity and efficiency over conventional, often complex, legal interfaces. This combination offers a distinct, user-friendly, and highly effective tool for IP protection.

## Problem Solving Approach
The tool directly tackles the labor-intensive and error-prone nature of drafting DMCA notices. By abstracting complex legal requirements into a simple input form and leveraging AI for formal language, it drastically reduces the time and effort needed to respond to infringements. This proactive approach helps organizations maintain control over their valuable digital assets.

## USP
Instant, AI-generated, and legally compliant DMCA takedown notices—empowering sports organizations with a rapid, professional, and actionable solution for comprehensive digital asset protection.

# Features
-   **Intuitive Input Form:** Collects essential details like rights holder name, asset description, original/infringing URLs, and date of infringement.
-   **AI-Powered DMCA Drafting:** Utilizes Google Gemini (`gemini-2.5-flash`) to generate formal, legally structured DMCA Section 512(c) takedown notices.
-   **Formatted Output Display:** Presents the generated legal notice with clear visual hierarchy, ready for immediate use.
-   **Loading & Error Handling:** Provides clear visual feedback during API requests and gracefully manages potential errors.
-   **Responsive Flat Design UI:** Delivers a professional, visually distinct user experience across all devices with a deep navy and electric gold color palette.

# Technologies
-   **Frontend Framework:** Next.js 16 (App Router)
-   **Language:** TypeScript
-   **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + Custom CSS Variables
-   **Icons:** lucide-react
-   **AI Integration:** Google Gemini API (`gemini-2.5-flash`)
-   **Runtime Environment:** Node.js 24.x
