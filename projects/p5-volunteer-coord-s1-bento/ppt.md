# Brief
Local social groups and NGOs struggle with disparate community data (paper surveys, field reports), hindering clear identification of urgent needs and efficient volunteer deployment. This leads to missed opportunities for timely intervention and suboptimal resource allocation.

# Opportunities
## Differentiation
NeedMapper stands out by combining an advanced AI-driven analysis engine with a highly visual and intuitive Bento Box Grid UI. Unlike manual methods or generic data entry systems, it specifically extracts, ranks, and categorizes urgent needs from raw text, providing a dynamic and comprehensive overview that traditional tools cannot match. Its focus on linking needs to volunteer coordination through clear, actionable data is unique.
## Problem Solving Approach
The system addresses the core problem by:
1.  **Centralizing Data**: Providing a single point for ingesting diverse, unstructured community data.
2.  **AI-Powered Analysis**: Utilizing Gemini AI to intelligently parse text, identify urgency signals, and structure information.
3.  **Prioritization & Visualization**: Presenting extracted needs with urgency scores, affected populations, and geographic clustering in a visually hierarchical Bento Box Grid, making complex data immediately digestible.
4.  **Actionable Insights**: Enabling NGOs to quickly understand critical areas and efficiently match volunteers to specific tasks.
## USP
Smart Resource Allocation — Data-Driven Volunteer Coordination for Social Impact. NeedMapper's unique selling proposition is its ability to rapidly convert chaotic, unstructured community data into precise, prioritized, and visually accessible information, empowering NGOs to make immediate, impactful decisions regarding volunteer deployment and resource allocation.

# Features
-   Unstructured text input for community surveys and field notes.
-   AI-powered extraction, categorization, and ranking of top 10 urgent needs.
-   Output includes urgency scores (1-10), estimated affected populations, and priority categories.
-   Geographic clustering and key urgency signal identification.
-   Interactive Bento Box Grid UI for dynamic display of results.
-   Responsive design for desktop and mobile access.
-   Clear loading indicators and robust error handling.
