PropagationMap is a Next.js 16 web application designed to combat widespread digital misappropriation of high-value sports media. It empowers sports organizations to proactively authenticate their digital assets and detect anomalies in content propagation. Users describe a media asset, and the Gemini AI simulates its likely spread across global platforms, identifying high-risk channels, potential anomaly signals, and providing a comprehensive timeline narrative. This innovative solution transforms a vast visibility gap into actionable intelligence for IP protection.
