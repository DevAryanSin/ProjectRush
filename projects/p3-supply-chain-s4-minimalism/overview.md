ETA Shield is a Next.js 16 web application designed to empower logistics professionals with preemptive disruption detection and dynamic route optimization. It allows users to input shipment details and identified disruptions to instantly receive a revised ETA, a detailed impact assessment, and a draft communication for stakeholders. By leveraging the Gemini AI model, the system analyzes complex transit data to mitigate cascading delays and enhance supply chain resilience, offering real-time intelligence in a minimalist, intuitive interface.
