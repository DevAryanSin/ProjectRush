DisruptRadar is a Next.js 16 web application designed to preemptively detect and mitigate supply chain disruptions. It provides users with a disruption risk score, identifies top risk factors, and offers specific mitigation recommendations for their shipment routes. By leveraging Gemini AI to analyze complex transit data, the system aims to prevent localized bottlenecks from escalating into broader delays, ensuring more resilient and dynamic logistics. The interface utilizes a modern, information-dense Bento Box Grid UI for optimal clarity.
