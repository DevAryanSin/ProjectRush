EthicsCheck is an AI ethics compliance auditor designed to identify and mitigate bias in automated decision-making systems. It allows organizations to describe their AI system, then leverages Gemini AI to generate a detailed compliance checklist mapped to key standards like the EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, and IEEE 7010. This solution provides clear status indicators (pass/fail/needs-review) to proactively measure, flag, and fix harmful bias before AI systems impact real people.
