This solution delivers an AI fairness auditing tool, "FairnessAudit," built with Next.js 16 and Tailwind CSS, styled using Glassmorphism. Users describe their ML models and training data, which is then sent to Google's Gemini AI. Gemini generates a comprehensive fairness audit report, displayed in a structured, accessible, and visually appealing format, empowering organizations to proactively identify and mitigate algorithmic bias.
